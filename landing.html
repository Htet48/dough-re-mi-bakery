What It Does

Everything your team needs,
nothing they don't

From morning stock assignment to end-of-day reporting — every part of your delivery sales process, covered.

📊
Live Admin Dashboard
See your entire business at a glance — updated automatically every minute, no manual refresh needed.
  • Today's total revenue and shops visited
  • Performance summary per delivery car
  • Best-selling products of the day
  • Returns and discounts tracked separately
📋
Morning Stock Assignment
Admin allocates the right products to each car route every morning — quickly and accurately.
  • Assign by scanning product barcodes
  • Or type quantities manually
  • Sold-out products hide automatically
  • Over-selling is blocked at the source
🛒
Sales Recording on the Road
Salesperson records each shop's sale on their phone in seconds — simple, fast, and mistake-proof.
  • Each shop's discount applied automatically
  • Can't sell more than what's assigned
  • Tracks how much was sold at other stops
  • Search and filter products instantly
🧾
Instant Receipt Printing
Print a professional receipt for every customer right after the sale — no delays, no handwriting.
  • Works with standard 80mm thermal printers
  • Unique invoice number per transaction
  • Shows all items, returns, discount and total
  • Tracks how many times each receipt was printed
📷
Barcode Scanning
Speed up data entry with barcode scanning — no typing needed for product lookup.
  • Plug-and-play USB or wireless scanners
  • Camera scan directly on any mobile phone
  • Automatically opens return screen for sold-out items
  • Prevents scanning over-allocated products
🔄
Returns Management
Handle product returns cleanly — separate from sales, with its own tracking and reporting.
  • Enter returns per product per shop
  • Return-only screen for fully sold items
  • Both admin and salesperson can process returns
  • Return totals shown in all reports
📈
Sales Reports & Export
Admin reviews every transaction for any date — with export options for records and accounting.
  • Filter by date range or delivery car
  • See when each sale was saved and printed
  • Highlights over-allocation warnings
  • Export to Excel or save to Google Drive
🔐
Secure Role-Based Access
Admin and salesperson each see only what they need — nothing more, nothing less.
  • Separate login for admin and salesperson
  • Auto session timeout when left idle
  • Each salesperson linked to their own route
  • Data protected at every level
